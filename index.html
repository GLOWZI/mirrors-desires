To The Mirrors Of My Desires

It takes a village to raise a child & it takes numerous mirrors of their desires to nurture and materialize their dreams. I believe I might have done great deeds in past iterations of my life as the villages & mirrors raising and nurturing me have & are still holding me oh so tenderly! it is filled with gratefulness, awe, love, and pride that i dedicate this page to them, to you, to us.
